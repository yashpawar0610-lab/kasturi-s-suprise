June 8, 2026 ✨
Dear Kasturi,
Who would have thought that two complete strangers would become such an important part of each other's lives? It still feels unreal when I think about how one random text started everything. At that time, we were just two people who barely knew each other, and today, after all this time, you're one of the most precious people in my life. From strangers to best friends, from random conversations to endless calls, from sharing small daily updates to knowing almost every little thing about each other's lives—our journey has been nothing short of beautiful. We've truly become the ultimate gossip partners and crime partners. We've shared countless happy moments, laughed at the most random things, celebrated each other's achievements, and stood beside each other during difficult days too. We've had our fights as well—many of them, honestly—but somehow we always found our way back. And let's be real, most of the time I was the one convincing Madam because that's just how it works. 😂 But looking back now, even those silly arguments have become memories that make me smile because they only showed how much our friendship mattered. One thing you probably don't know is that before you came into my life, I was genuinely scared of trusting someone again. I had convinced myself that maybe having a close female best friend just wasn't written in my destiny. I was afraid of getting attached, afraid of being left behind, and afraid of investing my trust again. But then you came along and slowly, without even realizing it, you changed that fear into comfort. From our very first chats to getting to know each other better every day, you made friendship feel effortless and genuine. And how can I ever forget those days when your parents would go downstairs for a walk and you'd call me? We would talk endlessly about absolutely everything and nothing at the same time. Then suddenly, the moment you'd hear the sound of the door opening, the call would disappear in a second. Those moments were hilarious and are honestly some of my favorite memories. They were simple, but they were ours. Then came the time when you moved to Parul University in Vadodara. I won't lie—I was scared. A lot. Somewhere in my mind, there was this fear that distance changes people, that maybe this friendship would slowly fade away too. I remember thinking, "Chhod yaar, shayad meri kismat mein close female friendship hai hi nahi." But for once, life proved me completely wrong. Even though you don't believe in destiny, I still think destiny had something beautiful planned for us. Because despite the distance, nothing really changed. Our daily calls continued, our conversations continued, your random gossip sessions continued, and so did our friendship. And in this entire one-and-a-half-year journey, I've realized something very special—I met one of the most wonderful souls I could have ever asked for. A girl who listens, cares, understands, supports, annoys, argues, laughs, gossips, and somehow manages to make ordinary days feel special. You've become someone whose presence brings comfort, whose happiness genuinely matters to me, and whose friendship I treasure more than words can ever explain. Thank you for trusting me with your stories, your secrets, your worries, your excitement, and your dreams. Thank you for every late-night conversation, every random call, every laugh, every memory, and every moment you've been there when I needed someone. You have no idea how much your friendship means to me. You've given me countless reasons to smile and reminded me that some people enter our lives unexpectedly and become irreplaceable. And before I end this, there's something I want to say from the bottom of my heart. If I have ever hurt you, disappointed you, annoyed you, or said something that made you feel bad—even unintentionally—I am really, really, really very sorry. Please know that it was never my intention. You are someone I care about deeply, and the last thing I would ever want is to hurt you. Thank you for being patient with me, understanding me, and accepting me despite all my flaws. On your special day, I don't just wish for your happiness today—I wish for a lifetime filled with smiles, success, peace, love, endless laughter, and all the beautiful things your heart desires. I hope every dream you carry finds its way to reality. I hope you always stay the amazing, kind-hearted, caring, strong, and beautiful person you are. And most importantly, I hope that no matter where life takes us, this friendship continues to stay exactly what it is today—pure, genuine, and unbreakable. Happy Birthday to the girl who turned a random text into one of the most beautiful friendships of my life. ❤️ You'll always be my favorite gossip partner, my favorite headache, and one of the most important people I'll never want to lose. 🫶✨
With all my love and endless gossip,
Your Crime Partner & Best Friend 🤫🤞
